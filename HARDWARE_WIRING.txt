KOMPONEN YANG DIPERLUKAN:

1. External Power Supply:
   - Minimum 5V 2A untuk servo + buzzer
   - Lebih baik 12V 2A (servo 5V via regulator)
   - USB Power Bank TIDAK CUKUP!

2. MOSFET untuk Buzzer (pilih salah satu):
   Option A: IRF520N atau similar NPN MOSFET
   - Gate: ESP32 GPIO 27 (via 1kΩ resistor)
   - Drain: External 5V arah Buzzer+
   - Source: Buzzer- ke GND

   Option B: 2N2222 NPN Transistor
   - Base: ESP32 GPIO 27 (via 10kΩ resistor)
   - Collector: External 5V arah Buzzer+
   - Emitter: Buzzer- ke GND

3. Relay untuk Buzzer (simpler):
   - Coil: GPIO 27 (via 1kΩ)
   - Common: Buzzer+
   - Normally Open: External 5V
   - Normally Closed: GND

4. Resistor 470Ω (signal protection servo)

REFERENSI PIN CONFIGURATION:

ESP32 Pinout:
- GPIO 27: BUZZER_PIN (output)
- GPIO 13: SERVO_PIN (PWM output)
- GPIO 5 : TRIG_PIN (ultrasonic)
- GPIO 18: ECHO_PIN (ultrasonic)
- GPIO 26, 25, 33: LED_RED, LED_GREEN, LED_YELLOW (output)
